Hi. I'm Bruno Bodin.

Assistant professor at Yale-NUS, Singapore.
Previously, I was a research associate at the University of Edinburgh from 2014 to 2018 and a sofware engineer in the Kalray company (Orsay, France) from 2011 to 2014. I received M.S and Ph.D. in Computer Engineering from the University of Pierre et Marie Curie (Paris, France) in 2010 and 2013 respectively. My major research interests are dataflow programming and compilers, especially static analysis and scheduling. My application domains are signal processing and robotics.
